About the Author

Meet Dr. Hal H. Sacks

Dr. Hal H. Sacks

Visionary Leader. Prayer Mobilizer. Cultural Transformer.

Dr. Hal H. Sacks is the President and CEO of BridgeBuilders International Leadership Network, which he co-founded with his wife Cheryl in 1981. For over four decades, they've mobilized prayer and cultural transformation initiatives that have impacted millions.

Raised in an Orthodox Jewish home, Hal encountered his Messiah during the Jesus Movement of the 1970s— while working in television and motion pictures in Hollywood. That life-altering moment launched him into ministry and confirmed his lifelong calling.
